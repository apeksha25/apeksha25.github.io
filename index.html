About me

At first, I intended to be a designer but destiny had different plans and I ended up in Computer Science. I started to enjoy the journey of learning how to code and it was then that it struck me, I can still follow my passion and combine it with code-A Full Stack Web Designer!

A wanderer and explorer, I am still exploring the innumerable web technologies the world has to offer today. I am a Master’s student from the prestigious New York University majoring in Computer Sciences. I work on building websites that are functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive.

Moreover, I always try to be unique with my code and design. I also keep in mind the scalability and the need of a clean code that is both maintainable and readable.

In my free time, I enjoy being outdoors, travelling, listening to music,watching TV shows and reading books.

Techies.com

Full stack development of a social networking website with functionalities similar to Facebook.

Created a complete working social networking website with functionalities similar to Facebook. Worked on the entire schema, front-end modules, back-end functionalities and the software diagrams.

In a nutshell, this system supported the following three aspects: first, users who signed up for the service were able to post content, such as a profile, diary entries (also called notes or postings), and maybe multi-media items such as photos, audio or video, which were made accessible via a page on the site that was created for that user. Second, the users were able to define relationships with other users that they know or that they consider their friends. Users were also able to restrict content so that only their direct friends, or friends of these friends, or everybody, can access their content. Third, other users or visitors could browse and search for content, subject to the access restrictions selected by the users who posted the content. Users who visit another user’s page on the site were also able to leave greetings or comments about the user’s page design, appearance, and brains.

Coding Languages used: PHP, HTML5, CSS3, PostgreSQL
