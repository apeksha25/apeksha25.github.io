Techies.com

Full stack development of a social networking website with functionalities similar to Facebook.

Created a complete working social networking website with functionalities similar to Facebook. Worked on the entire schema, front-end modules, back-end functionalities and the software diagrams.

In a nutshell, this system supported the following three aspects: first, users who signed up for the service were able to post content, such as a profile, diary entries (also called notes or postings), and maybe multi-media items such as photos, audio or video, which were made accessible via a page on the site that was created for that user. Second, the users were able to define relationships with other users that they know or that they consider their friends. Alert Android App

App that can be used in cases of emergency or any potential danger.

This app was developed to help people in times of potential danger or threat.The main functionality of this app was to register as a user into the app who will then add the contacts of three people whom you feel will help you out in times of need and emergency. Whenever you are out and sense any potential threat, all you need to do is press the help button on the app. This app will then send your geographical location(including the latitude and longitude) along with a message asking for help to the three contacts you initially added. This message is sent thrice at a regular interval of five minutes so as to make sure the user hasn't missed out on the message.

A Scope for future improvement was to send an email if the person of contact was at office or unable to use his/her phone at that particular time.

Coding languages used: Android, Java
